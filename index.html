2 The Two Paths Learn

The Consultant Path

What you do: Help businesses understand and implement AI without building custom solutions.

  • AI Audits: Review a business's processes and identify where AI can help
  • Strategy Sessions: Create roadmaps for AI adoption
  • Tool Selection: Recommend the right AI tools for their needs
  • Training: Teach teams how to use AI effectively
💰 Typical Pricing

AI Audits: $1,500 - $5,000 | Strategy Packages: $5,000 - $15,000

The Builder Path

What you do: Create custom AI solutions and automations for businesses.

  • AI Chatbots: Customer service bots, lead capture, internal knowledge bases
  • Workflow Automation: Connect AI to existing business processes
  • Custom Agents: AI systems that perform specific business tasks
  • Voice AI: Phone systems, appointment booking, customer support
💰 Typical Pricing

Chatbots: $3,000 - $10,000 | Voice AI: $5,000 - $20,000 | Custom Agents: $10,000+

Which Path is Right for You?

Choose Consultant if:

  • You're good at explaining complex things simply
  • You have business experience or industry knowledge
  • You want to start earning faster (less technical setup)

Choose Builder if:

  • You enjoy technical problem-solving
  • You want to create products that scale
  • You're comfortable learning no-code/low-code tools
💡 Pro Tip

Many people start as consultants to understand client needs, then add building services later.

4 Getting Your First Client Do

The 9-Day First Client Method

  1. Day 1-2: List everyone you know - friends, family, ex-colleagues, LinkedIn connections
  2. Day 3-4: Identify which ones own businesses or work at companies that could use AI
  3. Day 5-6: Reach out with a simple, non-salesy message offering a free AI audit
  4. Day 7-8: Do the audit - find 3-5 ways AI could help their specific business
  5. Day 9: Present findings and offer to implement (paid project)
⚡ Why This Works

You're offering value first (free audit) and letting them see the opportunity before asking for money.

Simple Outreach Script

Copy and personalise this:

"Hey [Name], I've been diving deep into AI for business lately and I'm doing free AI audits for people I know to build my portfolio. Basically I'd look at your business and show you where AI could save you time or money. No strings attached - would you be interested?"

Key elements: Personal connection, offering value, no pressure, clear ask.
